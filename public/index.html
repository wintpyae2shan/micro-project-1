What is Football?

Football, also known as soccer, is the world's most popular sport, captivating billions of fans across every continent. It’s a game that combines skill, strategy, and endurance, played on a rectangular field with two teams of eleven players each. The aim is simple: score by getting the ball into the opponent’s goal.

Why it is so popular?

Football is celebrated for its accessibility—anyone can play with just a ball and some open space, making it a unifying force in communities around the globe. Major events like the FIFA World Cup showcase football’s universal appeal, drawing millions to cheer for their teams and players.
